Licensed Real Estate Broker · Florida

With over 23 years of experience in Florida real estate, Julio Cardoso has helped hundreds of families buy, sell, and invest across Miami-Dade, Broward, Orange, Osceola, and Brevard counties. He founded Elite Capital Realty Inc. to build a brokerage that puts clients — and agents — first.

Julio is a member of the Miami Association of Realtors, Orlando Regional Realtor Association, and Space Coast Association of Realtors. Fluent in English and Spanish, he's known for making the complex process of buying or selling feel simple and stress-free.
